# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all people who share our passion for public speaking and AI-powered education.

This Code of Conduct applies to all community interactions including:
- GitHub repositories and issues
- Pull requests and code reviews
- Discussions and forums
- Online and in-person events

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- **Being respectful** - Treat all community members with respect and dignity
- **Being inclusive** - Welcome people of all backgrounds, experiences, and perspectives
- **Being constructive** - Provide helpful feedback and support to others
- **Being patient** - Remember that everyone is learning and growing
- **Using inclusive language** - Avoid language that is disrespectful or exclusionary
- **Focusing on the project** - Keep discussions relevant to the project and community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- **Harassment** - Unwelcome comments, jokes, or conduct related to identity
- **Discrimination** - Treating people unfairly based on any personal characteristic
- **Abuse** - Hostile, insulting, or demeaning behavior or language
- **Trolling** - Deliberate attempts to provoke or upset community members
- **Privacy violations** - Sharing others' private information without consent
- **Spam** - Repeatedly posting off-topic or promotional content

### Response
When a Code of Conduct violation is reported:

1. **Investigation** - Maintainers will promptly investigate the report
2. **Communication** - The reporter will receive a response within 7 days
3. **Action** - Appropriate action will be taken based on the severity
4. **Confidentiality** - We'll protect the privacy of those involved

### Consequences

Depending on severity, consequences may include:
- **Warning** - First-time minor violations
- **Temporary Ban** - Repeated violations or serious misconduct (1-7 days)
- **Permanent Ban** - Severe or repeated harassment

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces and interactions, including online and offline.
